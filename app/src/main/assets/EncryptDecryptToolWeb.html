How to Encrypt
1
Click Encrypt and enter the text you want to protect.
2
Press Submit.
3
Enter a strong password to lock the message.
4
Press Submit again. Copy the result.
How to Decrypt
1
Click Decrypt and paste the encrypted Base64 text.
2
Press Submit.
3
Enter the password used during encryption.
4
Press Submit again to reveal the original text.
⚠️ If you lose your password, there is no recovery option. Keep it safe.
Clear Button
Press Clear at any time to reset all fields, the output window, and your clipboard.
A confirmation prompt will appear before anything is cleared.
Encryption
AES-256-GCM — authenticated encryption, protects confidentiality and detects tampering.
PBKDF2-SHA256 (100,000 iterations) — hardens your password against brute-force attacks.
Random salt & IV per session — the same text never produces the same ciphertext.
Privacy & Storage
Fully offline. Network access is blocked by Content Security Policy — nothing is sent anywhere.
Zero storage. No cookies, localStorage, or saved passwords are ever used.
Clipboard. Other apps may read clipboard contents — use Copy only when appropriate.
Once text leaves this tool — via clipboard, email, or file — it is outside this tool's control.
Open Source & Transparency
This app is fully open source. Anyone can inspect, audit, and verify the complete source code.
Because this is a security tool, full transparency is a core principle — you should never have to trust blindly.

Overview
Encrypt & Decrypt Data Utility Tool is developed and maintained by Amanda Hernow. This app does not collect, transmit, or store any data of any kind. Everything stays on your device.
Data Collection
No personal information, no usage statistics, no analytics, no crash reports, and no identifiers of any kind are gathered, stored, or transmitted.
Encryption and Decryption
All cryptographic operations are performed entirely on your device using the Web Crypto API.
The text you enter, the password you use, and the encrypted output never leave your device through this app.
Clipboard Access
Paste reads your clipboard to fill the input field.
Copy writes the result to your clipboard.
Clear clears your clipboard.
No clipboard content is stored, transmitted, or accessed at any other time.
Network Access
The app makes no network requests, does not contact any server, and does not communicate with any external service. All operations are fully offline.
Third-Party Services
No third-party SDKs, advertising networks, analytics platforms, crash reporting tools, or tracking services are integrated.
Device Permissions
The app declares no device permissions. No access to camera, microphone, location, contacts, storage, or other device resources is requested or used.
Data Storage
No passwords, plaintext, ciphertext, or other data is written to device storage or any persistent location.
When you close the app, no trace of your session remains.
Children
The app does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. Because it collects no data at all, it poses no privacy risk to users of any age.
