Privacy Policy — Encrypt & Decrypt Data Utility Tool

Last updated: 25 March 2026

Encrypt & Decrypt Data Utility Tool ("the App") is developed and maintained by Amanda Hernow.

No data is collected. The App does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information, usage data, or analytics of any kind. All encryption and decryption operations are performed entirely on your device. No data ever leaves your device through the App.

No internet access. The App requires no internet connection and makes no network requests.

No third-party services. The App does not integrate any advertising, analytics, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs.

Permissions. The App requests no device permissions.

Children. The App does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.
