Encrypt & Decrypt Data Utility Tool — Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 March 2026

Overview

Encrypt & Decrypt Data Utility Tool ("the App") is developed and maintained by Amanda Hernow. This policy describes how the App handles your data. The short version is: it does not collect, transmit, or store any data of any kind. Everything stays on your device.

Data Collection

The App collects no data whatsoever. No personal information, no usage statistics, no analytics, no crash reports, and no identifiers of any kind are gathered, stored, or transmitted — ever.

Encryption and Decryption Operations

All cryptographic operations — encryption, decryption, key derivation — are performed entirely on your device using the Web Crypto API. The text you enter, the password you use, and the encrypted output never leave your device through the App.

Clipboard Access

The App interacts with your clipboard only through direct user action and only in the following three ways:

When you tap Paste, the App reads your clipboard to fill the input field. When you tap Copy, the App writes the result to your clipboard. When you tap Clear, the App clears your clipboard.

No clipboard content is stored, transmitted, or accessed at any other time. These operations require no system permission declarations on Android.

Network Access

The App makes no network requests. It does not connect to the internet, contact any server, or communicate with any external service. All operations are fully offline.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs, advertising networks, analytics platforms, crash reporting tools, or tracking services of any kind.

Device Permissions

The App declares no device permissions in its manifest. No access to camera, microphone, location, contacts, storage, or any other device resource is requested or used.

Data Storage

The App stores nothing on your device. No passwords, plaintext, ciphertext, or any other data is written to device storage, shared preferences, or any persistent location. When you close the App, no trace of your session remains.

Children

The App does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because the App collects no data at all, it poses no privacy risk to users of any age.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be published at this URL with an updated date at the top. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact
